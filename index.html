About Us



Welcome to PG Homes, your trusted partner in finding the perfect accommodation that feels like home. We understand that moving to a new city for studies or work can be both exciting and challenging. That’s why we are dedicated to providing you with comfortable, secure, and affordable paying guest (PG) accommodations tailored to meet your needs.

Who We Are

At PG Homes, we are more than just a PG accommodation provider; we are a community. Founded by a team of professionals who have experienced the highs and lows of relocating, our mission is to ease your transition by offering a seamless living experience. With years of expertise in the hospitality and real estate sectors, we bring you the best of both worlds: the warmth of a home and the convenience of modern amenities.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to provide high-quality, hassle-free living spaces where you can thrive. Whether you are a student, a working professional, or someone new to the city, we strive to make your stay as comfortable and enjoyable as possible. We are committed to creating a supportive environment that fosters personal growth, well-being, and a sense of community.

What We Offer

  • Comfortable Living Spaces: Our PG accommodations are designed to offer you the utmost comfort. Each room is fully furnished with cozy beds, study tables, ample storage, and tasteful decor.
  • Modern Amenities: Enjoy high-speed Wi-Fi, laundry facilities, 24/7 security, housekeeping services, and more. We ensure that all our properties are well-maintained and equipped with the latest amenities to make your stay pleasant.
  • Prime Locations: All our PGs are strategically located near major educational institutions, business hubs, and public transport, making your daily commute easy and convenient.
  • Community Engagement: We believe in fostering a sense of belonging. Our properties regularly host social events, workshops, and activities that help you connect with fellow residents and build lasting friendships.
  • Affordable Pricing: Quality living doesn’t have to break the bank. We offer competitive pricing and flexible payment options to suit your budget.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We believe in transparent dealings and building trust with our residents.
  • Customer-Centric: Your comfort and satisfaction are our top priorities.
  • Innovation: Continuously improving our services and facilities to meet your evolving needs.
  • Community: Creating a supportive and inclusive environment where everyone feels at home.
